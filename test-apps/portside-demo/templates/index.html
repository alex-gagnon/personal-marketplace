About Us

Portside Behavior Consulting was founded by Dr. Mae Calloway, a certified applied animal behaviorist (CAAB) with over 15 years of experience helping dogs and their families thrive. Our approach is force-free and grounded in the science of learning theory — no punishment, no fear.

Whether your dog is struggling with separation anxiety, leash reactivity, aggression, or simply needs a solid foundation of manners, we build a personalized plan that works for your household.
